Code Label Description
M1 Measure refusal - no reason provided Participant refused to do the measure either during the assessment or prior.
M2 No show Participant did not show up for their scheduled appointment and was not able to be rebooked. E.g. participant did not verbally refuse to come, but they did not show, for e.g. the blood draw and you are now unable to reach them to re-book.
M3 Research assistant forgot RA forgot to do one or more tasks and is unable to contact the participant to obtain the information.
M4 Uncontrollable circumstance Circumstances beyond the control of the site, e.g. faulty equipment. If such circumstances occur sites will attempt to recollect the sample or do the assessment later but if this is impossible then this code will be used.
M5 Participant dropped out The participant has been dropped from the study.
